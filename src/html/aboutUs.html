About Us

The E-Commerce Group is one of India’s leading digital commerce entities and includes group companies. Started in 2007, E-Commerce has enabled millions of sellers, merchants, and small businesses to participate in India's digital commerce revolution With a registered customer base of more than 500 million, E-Commerce's marketplace offers over 150 million products across 80+ categories. Today, there are over 14 lakh sellers on the platform. With a focus on empowering and delighting every Indian by delivering value through technology and innovation, E-Commerce has created lakhs of jobs in the ecosystem while empowering generations of entrepreneurs and MSMEs. E-Commerce is known for pioneering services such as Cash on Delivery, No Cost EMI and easy returns, which are customer-centric innovations that have made online shopping more accessible and affordable for millions of Indians. In 2024, E-Commerce also introduced the E-Commerce UPI handle to further enhance its digital payment offerings for all customers and further India’s digital economy vision.
